How to get Google drive share link:

Step 1:

Go to Google Drive and right click the file that you want to share. Then click on "Share...".



Step 2:

On the window that comes up, click "Get shareable link".



Step 3:

Make sure the dropdown is set to one of the "Anyone with the link..." or "Anyone on the internet..." options, then click "Copy link".
